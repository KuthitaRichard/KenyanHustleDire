Our Journey

From humble beginnings to empowering thousands across Africa

2018

The Beginning

Founded in Nairobi with a simple goal: help local entrepreneurs access the resources they need to succeed. Started with just 5 members and big dreams.

2020

Digital Expansion

Launched our digital platform, enabling us to reach entrepreneurs across Kenya and neighboring countries. Grew to over 500 active members.

2022

Regional Growth

Expanded operations to Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda. Established partnerships with major financial institutions and international development organizations.

2024

Innovation Hub

Opened our flagship innovation hub in Nairobi, providing state-of-the-art facilities for startups and established businesses alike. Now supporting over 1,500 entrepreneurs.
