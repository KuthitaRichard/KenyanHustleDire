What services does Kenyan Hustle Dire offer?

+

We offer comprehensive business support services including:

  • Business Consulting: Strategic planning, market research, and business development
  • Digital Marketing: Social media management, content creation, SEO, and online advertising
  • Funding Support: Investor connections, grant applications, and loan facilitation
  • Training Programs: Entrepreneurship bootcamps, digital literacy, and skill development
  • Technology Solutions: Website development, mobile apps, and business automation
  • Community & Networking: Access to our entrepreneur network and mentorship programs

Do you work with businesses outside Kenya?

+

Yes! While we're based in Nairobi, we work with entrepreneurs and businesses across East Africa including Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, and beyond. We provide remote support through video consultations, online training programs, and digital collaboration tools.

What industries do you specialize in?

+

We work across various industries with particular expertise in:

  • Agriculture and Agribusiness
  • Technology and Fintech
  • Retail and E-commerce
  • Manufacturing and Production
  • Services and Consulting
  • Tourism and Hospitality
  • Health and Wellness
  • Education and Training

How much do your services cost?

+

Our pricing varies based on the service and scope of work:

  • Technology Solutions: From KSh 50,000 per project
  • Community Membership: KSh 2,000 per month
  • Funding Support: 5% success fee on funding secured

Do you offer payment plans or flexible pricing?

Yes! We understand that cash flow can be challenging for growing businesses. We offer:

  • Success-based pricing for certain services
  • Equity partnerships for high-potential startups
  • Discounted rates for early-stage businesses
  • Group rates for training programs

+

Results timelines vary by service:

What's your typical process for new clients?

+

Our proven 4-step process ensures your success:

  1. Discovery Call (Free): We understand your business, goals, and challenges
  2. Custom Strategy: We develop a tailored plan and recommend the best services
  3. Implementation: Our expert team works with you to execute solutions
  4. Ongoing Support: Continuous monitoring, optimization, and growth support

The entire onboarding process typically takes 1-2 weeks from initial contact to project kickoff.
